ABOUT

We are a group of three young sports journalists born in Buenos Aires, who in 2018 graduated and made the decision to start a journalistic venture about the most important basketball league in the world. Due to the subsequent pandemic, we reimagined Goaltending as a site where we could offer articles about the NBA, taking advantage of the lack of products that exist in our country. Since then, our website has not stopped growing and we still have many new features to implement to our site. Below you will find our social networks for any questions.
